Simple Omelette Recipe

This classic omelette recipe is quick, easy, and perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Optional fillings: cheese, ham, vegetables

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs with milk, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture and cook until set.
  4. Add optional fillings if desired.
  5. Fold the omelette and serve warm.
